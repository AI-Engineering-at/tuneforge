# TuneForge Compliance Statement
- Language: EN
- Audience: Public
- Last Sync: 2026-03-19

TuneForge provides audit-ready engineering documentation for Austria, DSGVO, and the EU AI Act.

## What TuneForge Does Claim

- documented provenance and release evidence
- traceable benchmark and validation artifacts
- bilingual engineering guidance for governance preparation
- explicit templates for model, data, release, and logging documentation

## What TuneForge Does Not Claim

- not legal advice
- not a compliance certification
- not an automatic proof that a model is lawful for every Austrian or EU use case

Use TuneForge as engineering support for governance review, DPIA or VVT preparation, traceability, and publication discipline.
