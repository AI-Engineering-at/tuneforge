# TuneForge: a benchmark-first self-finetune stack for real hardware

TuneForge is the public framework. TuneForge Studio is the Docker-first product surface. Together they turn local fine-tuning, benchmark evidence, and model publishing into a reproducible workflow instead of an ad hoc notebook trail.

As of March 17, 2026, the public surface includes:

- benchmark-first QLoRA configs
- hybrid `peft_trl` and `unsloth` backends
- Hugging Face release bundles
- Ollama-compatible packaging targets
- governance and compliance documentation for DSGVO-aware teams

The public repo is the lead magnet. The internal leverage stays in open-notebook: experiment notes, review decisions, and content derivations for wiki, blog, and social.
