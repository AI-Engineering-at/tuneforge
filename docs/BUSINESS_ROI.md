# Business ROI

TuneForge matters when the cost of repeated API usage, privacy exposure, or inconsistent output is higher than the cost of one governed local fine-tune cycle.

## Why teams adopt it

- lower recurring inference cost after training
- lower privacy exposure for sensitive internal workflows
- predictable latency on local infrastructure
- public benchmark evidence instead of vague model claims

## Lead Magnet vs Product

- public lead magnet: framework, Docker runtime, docs, benchmark reports, release examples
- product leverage: repeatable release discipline, stronger hardware support, internal research knowledge, and governed publishing workflows
